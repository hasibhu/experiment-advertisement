BIOLOGICAL MOTION
EXPERIMENT
Help us understand how people perceive motion created by the human body.

ABOUT THE STUDY

You will watch walking avatars of moving dot displays (biological motion) and complete simple tasks about what you see.

💻

WHAT YOU’LL DO

  • View avatars of moving dots
  • Answer questions on each trial
  • The session takes about 30 minutes
  • Complete the session entirely in-lab on a computer
👥

WHO CAN PARTICIPATE?

We are looking for adults (18+) with normal or corrected-to-normal vision. No prior experience is needed!

🎁
